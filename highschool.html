Northwest High School

August 2019 --- May 2023

My high school years were filled with achievements and memorable experiences. I consistently earned a spot on the Honor Roll and was awarded the Academic Letter every year. Beyond academics, I participated in show choir and musical performances, earning recognition such as the Nebraska All-State Choir and the Top 15% of Class Award in 2023. These years were a blend of learning, growth, and unforgettable moments that shaped my journey.

Intramural Basketball

Playing IBA basketball was one of my favorite activities. We had to wake up early before school to scrimmage against other teams, which made for some fun and competitive mornings. All the hard work paid off when we competed in the big tournament—and our team came out on top as champions!
