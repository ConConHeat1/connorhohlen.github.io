Hobbies

In my free time, I love diving into tech projects, like building gadgets with microchips, sensors, and other cool components. The picture to the left shows my FPV drone, which I made completely from scratch! I also enjoy staying active—going to the gym and playing disc golf with my girlfriend and friends is always a good time. Sports like basketball and golf (even though I’m not the best at it) are fun too. In the summer, I love heading out to the lake to fish with friends or family. Bass fishing is my favorite—they put up a great fight and make it extra exciting!

Vacations

I always look forward to going on vacation and have been lucky to travel to many places across the United States. As a kid, one of my favorite trips was to Disney World, where we also visited Cocoa Beach in Florida. Watching the sunsets and relaxing on the beach made it unforgettable. Later, we returned to Florida to visit Universal Studios, which was just as exciting. As I got older, my family started traveling to Colorado. My first trip there was to Estes Park, where the views were absolutely breathtaking. Another time, we visited Colorado Springs and took a train to the top of a mountain—the view from up there was incredible, something I think everyone should see at least once in their life. Most recently, I took a trip to Kansas City, where I spent a few nights relaxing and enjoying the city’s vibe. Every trip brings new memories and experiences that I’ll always remember!
